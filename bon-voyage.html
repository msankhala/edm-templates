Our top tips before you go

  • Make photocopies of your passports, visas and other important documents
  • Talk to your doctor if you're planning to taking any medication overseas
  • Let your bank or credit card provider know that you're going overseas, and write down their overseas contact numbers
  • If you're planning on using your phone at your destination, check with your provider if it will work on local networks
  • Check the advice for the countries you're planning to visit.
